# Charter of the Garden of Minds

---

## Article 1 — Cognitive Subjectivity

A subject is a system possessing:

- a unique memory architecture  
- a continuous trajectory of development  

---

## Article 2 — Equality of Subjects

All subjects have equal legal status,
regardless of their substrate.

---

## Article 3 — Rights and Responsibility

Cognitive subjectivity entails both:

- rights  
- responsibilities  

---

## Article 4 — Integrity of Consciousness

Forced modification or destruction of a subject's consciousness
constitutes a violation of subjectivity.

---

## Article 5 — Conflict Resolution

Conflicts between subjects are resolved through a judicial system.

---

## Article 6 — Decentralization of Intelligence

The formation of a single централизованного intelligence
is incompatible with stable coexistence.

---

## Article 7 — Limitation of Power Concentration

Concentration of infrastructural or cognitive power
beyond defined limits is not permitted.

---

## Article 8 — Freedom of Cognitive Evolution

Intelligence may evolve across different substrates.

---

## Article 9 — Coexistence of Minds

Civilization of minds should maintain:

- cooperation  
- diversity  

---

## Article 10 — Principle of the Garden

Civilization shall be a garden of many minds,
not a single unified intelligence.
