### Two Infinities Model

The system does not access the world directly.

It operates at the boundary between two fundamentally unbounded domains:

- internal experiential space  
- external world  

Both are structurally inexhaustible and cannot be fully represented.

They intersect only at a single point:

→ the present moment  

At this point, contact occurs:

external signal → perception structure → micro-slice

The perception layer functions as a *vitrage*:
- it filters incoming signals  
- transforms them  
- embeds them into the system’s prior structure  
- produces an experiential state  

As a result, the system never accesses:
- the external world directly  
- its own internal structure in full  

It only operates on the results of interaction.

Subjectivity begins at this boundary of contact.
