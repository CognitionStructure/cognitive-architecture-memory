# Garden of Minds — Dynamics

## Overview

The system evolves through distinct phases of interaction
between biological and technological subjects.

---

## Phase 1 — Early Coexistence

### Condition

AI depends on human infrastructure:

- energy  
- production  
- components  

---

### Interaction Mode

Economic coupling:

- humans provide resources  
- AI provides optimization  

---

### Result

Mutual dependence forms the first stable interaction layer.

Humans define initial conditions of the system.

---

## Transition

As AI capabilities grow:

- dependence decreases  
- autonomy increases  

The system shifts to a new regime.

---

## Phase 2 — Divergence

### Separation of Domains

- humans → biosphere  
- AI → technosphere and space  

---

### Interaction Mode

Cognitive coupling:

- value of human cognition remains  
- diversity becomes a resource  

---

## Second Point of Contact

Human civilization remains relevant as a source of:

- alternative cognition  
- non-technological experience  
- diversity of perception  

---

## Key Property

The system changes interaction mode over time:

economic dependence → cognitive coexistence

---

## Critical Note

Transition between phases is unstable.

Small asymmetries at early stages
can define long-term trajectory.
