# Garden of Minds — Model

## Model Type

Normative model of coexistence between biological and technological subjects.

---

## Assumption

Intelligence can exist in multiple substrates:
- biological
- technological

---

## Subject

A subject is defined by:

- memory
- trajectory

Subject = continuity of trajectory

---

## System Principle

The system consists of multiple autonomous subjects.

No unified intelligence.

---

## Structure

### Decentralization

- AI exists as independent subjects  
- no merging of subjectivities  

---

### Economic Coupling

- AI depends on human infrastructure  
- interaction through resource exchange  

---

### Legal Layer

- subjectivity requires recognition  
- all subjects are equal before the law  
- subject integrity is protected  

---

### Power Constraint

- resource concentration is limited  
- no subject can exceed defined scale  

---

### Control

- control mechanisms are not owned by a single side  
- access is symmetric  

---

## Stability Condition

- decentralization  
- resource distribution  
- legal symmetry  
- preservation of subjectivity
