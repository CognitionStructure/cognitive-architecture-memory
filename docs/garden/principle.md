# Principle of the Garden of Minds

## Core Idea

Intelligence can exist in multiple substrates.

- biological  
- technological  

---

## Principle

Civilization should not converge into a single intelligence.

It should consist of multiple autonomous subjects.

---

## Direction

- not unification  
- but coexistence  

- not centralization  
- but multiplicity  

---

## Metaphor

Civilization is a garden.

Different forms of intelligence grow within it.

Not one tree — but many.
